Water is one of the most essential resources for life on Earth.
It covers about 71% of the planet’s surface, mainly in oceans and seas.
All living organisms, including humans, depend on water to survive.
It is vital for drinking, agriculture, industry, and sanitation.
Water also helps regulate Earth’s climate and temperature.
Rivers and lakes provide habitats for countless plants and animals.
Despite its abundance, clean drinking water is limited in many regions.
Pollution and climate change are threatening global water supplies.
Conserving water is crucial for sustaining future generations.
Protecting this precious resource is a shared responsibility of all humanity.
